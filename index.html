about me

Giole Marrino

Music Producer

Giole Marrino is a classically trained flutist who graduated from the Faculty of Music in Belgrade in 2009.

Since then, he has been teaching flute at a music school. Shortly after graduating, he began learning guitar and has played and performed in a band since 2010. In 2015, he started composing and producing music for licensing platforms, gradually developing his style and sound. In recent years, he has focused on creating original music, blending funk, electronic, lo-fi, jazz-swing, and rock’n’roll. This fusion comes to life in his debut album Someplace, released in April 2025. He is currently working on new material, continuing to explore and evolve his sound.
